Drawing over things is a way to get inside them.

COMPOSITE

Based on the Cognitive Interview used by police, FBI, and forensic artists to acquire information from a witness about a suspect, Jill Magid wrote a letter asking people she has known intimately to recall her face in detail.

She wrote a second letter to forensic artists explaining that there is a woman who is absent, that she can provide her description, and require their help to realize her.
