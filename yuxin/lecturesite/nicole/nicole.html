Nicole Fenton
is an independent editor, writer, and content strategist in Brooklyn.She manages content projects of all sizes and teaches writing as a design practice. She has worked at large social networks, startup incubators and design studios.she served as communications lead for five years and helped launch the original iPhone and iPad.

Nicole believe goods writing is clear, honest, and friendly. she recently co-wrote a book about that called Nicely Said: Writing for the Web with Style and Purpose with Kate Kiefer Lee.
