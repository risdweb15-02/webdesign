Roman Mars
is the creator of 99% Invisible, a short radio show about design and architecture. Fast Company named him one of 100 Most Creative People in 2013. He is also the host and program director of PRX Remix, a 24-hour, innovative public radio story stream broadcast on XM 123 and public radio stations across the country.

99% Invisible explores all things design, from the secret stories behind everyday objects like the teddy bear to historical tales like the rise and fall of pneumatic tubes to personal stories of connection with place and space. But the show's real theme is the same one that makes any good radio compelling: the human experience. With over 30 million downloads, the 99% Invisible podcast is one of the most popular programs on iTunes. The show started life as a project of KALW public radio in San Francisco and the American Institute of Architects in San Francisco.
