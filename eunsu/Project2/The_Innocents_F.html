Introduction



     Know the Cases

There have been 325 post-conviction DNA exonerations in United States history. These stories are becoming more familiar as more innocent people gain their freedom through postconviction testing.



Understand the Causes

As the pace of DNA exonerations has grown across the country in recent years, wrongful convictions have revealed disturbing fissures and trends in our criminal justice system. Together, these cases show us how the criminal justice system is broken – and how urgently it needs to be fixed.



The Innocence Project is a national litigation and public polict organization dedicated to oxonerating wrongfully convicted individuals.
