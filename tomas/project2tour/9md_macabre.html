The Macabre Treasury

This shewn was the artist's portent of the future and reenactment of the present that was, in that time. The display of careful taxonomy was of a clever commentary with dark reservations ; in the careful display, we found both the fruits of knowledge and the extinction of its source. The Idea presented herein is of a sort that cannot abate its own medium, and so we are forced to conclude it was the failed Ouroboros, consuming it's own beginning.
