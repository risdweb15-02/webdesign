Architecture Dinsmoor House

Eames & Walsh

Dinsmoor House, 335 Bristol Road, Webster Groves, Missouri

1936, for Daniel S. & Jean Dinsmoor.

Cost $11,000.

Dinsmoor was an assistant manager at the Monsanto Chemical Co.

After a sojourn in Mexico, Eames became the partner of Robert P. Walsh (1898-1964), who was the son of Robert W. Walsh (1855-1928) of Widmann, Walsh & Boisselier, and the grandson of Thomas Waryng Walsh (1827-1890), one of the foremost architects of 19th-century St. Louis.
