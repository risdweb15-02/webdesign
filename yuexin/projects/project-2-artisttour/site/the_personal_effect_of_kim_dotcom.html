Simon Denny assembles a series of objects in a sculptural visualisation of this list. Images, files, and company data, from a life-sized Predator statue to three cubic meters of cash, from a luxury ‘work-bed’ to examples of artwork collected by Dotcom, will be included amongst the 110 items on the list. The result is a large installation that Denny describes as a ‘collection of copies, rip-offs and limitations of the ‘real’ contraband.’
