Things to Say

Things to Say documents a series of collected drawings produced in collaboration with invited guests to perform with Viktor every Thursday evening throughout the duration of the exhibition A Recent History Of Writing And Drawing at the ICA in London in 2008.

The book was produced by replacing the blade in the vinyl cutter used in Empty Words with a felt-tip pen. A selection of the vector drawings originally executed by Viktor was then drawn, true to the scale of the chalk.

The resulting drawings were scanned and printed with white ink on black paper, to reference the original chalk drawings.
