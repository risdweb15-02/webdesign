Magazine - on Race
"In the piece called 'What if...? the celebrities were chosen because they would be recognized by their faces only - globally. The idea was that if you could separate the person from their race you could then figure out how you feel about the race versus the person. So when you look at the black Queen, you think 'does she dance better? Is Buckingham Palace playing cool Caribbean music at receptions?' And as you explore these questions you become conscious of your own racism."
Tibor Kalman
