James Turrell

March 20, 6:30 PM, Granoff Center

James Turrell is best known for his explorations of perception, light, color and space that engage viewers with the limits and wonders of human perception. He considers the sky as his studio, material and canvas.

New York critic Calvin Thompkins writes, "His work is not about light, or a record of light; it is light — the physical presence of light made manifest in sensory form."
