About

Shared Voices: Time & Environment brings together 3 contemporary artists: Dan Flavin, James Turrell and Hiroshi Sugimoto — visionaries who explore concepts of light, space, color and perception. Collectively, their work has influenced the future of art, design and architecture. The event involves lectures and installations done by each artist and promotes students to consider ways in which this event can inspire them.

This event runs from March 19-20, 2015 and is open to all RISD students.
