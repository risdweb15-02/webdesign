Dan Flavin

March 19, 6:30 PM, Chace Center

Dan Flavin is a minimalist artist best known for his sculptural installations made of fluorescent light fixtures. He is acknowledged for his mastery of dimensional art dealing with illusionism and dynamism. He is considered one of the inventors of "installation art" as well as minimalism.

His sophisticated use of color and light in architecture has influenced twentieth century art and design.
