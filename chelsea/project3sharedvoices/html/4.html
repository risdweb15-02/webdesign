Hiroshi Sugimoto

March 21, 6:30 PM, RISD Auditorium

Hiroshi Sugimoto is a Japanese artist best known for his photography dealing with the expression of transience. His work involves minimal imagery of sea scapes, theaters and architecture as well as detailed dioramas. He illustrates concepts of time, space, culture and reality.
