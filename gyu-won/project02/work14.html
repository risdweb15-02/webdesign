Freshkills Park

December 2007

Fresh Kills, one of the world’s largest landfills, will be cleaned up and transformed into
a 2,400 acre park over the next thirty years. Project Projects is working with landscape architects Field Operations and New York City Department of Parks on an ongoing basis to design an identity and signage system which reuses existing structural elements to signal the park’s long-term transformation.
