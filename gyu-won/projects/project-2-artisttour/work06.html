Tracing Istanbul

September 2009
10 x 7 inches, 192 pages, hardcover

Tracing Istanbul (from the air) presents a selection of aerial photographs by Oğuz
Meriç depicting a surprising array of landscapes and conditions within the space of the city. The book situates these photographs within a running dialogue between three urban theorists which visually suggests the complex and interwoven quality of the
urban fabric.
